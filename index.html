Dehydrated Fruits and Vegetables

Our range features fruit powders and slices including tamarind, papaya, mango, pineapple, banana, apple, pomegranate, strawberry, and chikoo. Each offers unique flavor and nutrition—perfect for use in snacks, drinks, supplements, baking, and more.

We offer a wide range of vegetable powders, flakes, and granules—including red and white onion, garlic, ginger, potato, green chili, carrot, cabbage, tomato, and lemon. Perfect for adding flavor and nutrition to soups, snacks, sauces, seasonings, and beverages.
